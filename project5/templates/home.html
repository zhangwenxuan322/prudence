Welcome to Prudence

Prudence is a web-based Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) platform designed to assist organizations in identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks. This system leverages Django as its backend framework and JavaScript for dynamic front-end interactions, ensuring a responsive and intuitive user experience. Prudence provides comprehensive tools for managing risks and controls, evaluating inherent and residual risks, monitoring control effectiveness, and ensuring compliance with ISO 31000 standards.
